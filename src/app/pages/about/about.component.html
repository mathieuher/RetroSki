Using the service
  • RetroSki is an online game designed for all ski lovers looking for a retro 2D experience on the slopes.
  • By playing, you agree to respect fair play, enjoy the ride, and avoid any unfair advantage.
  • The game is constantly evolving, new features, events, and optimizations will hit the slopes over time!
Accounts & data
  • Your RetroSki account is personal, keep it safe and secure to enjoy uninterrupted access.
  • Online features require a verified account.
  • We do our best to keep your data safe, but remember that leaderboard stats and online races are public.
Memberships & purchases
  • Premium accounts unlock extra perks, giving you more ways to share the slopes with friends.
  • Any purchase you make is final and non-refundable.
  • Memberships do not auto-renew, you decide when to extend your premium advantages!
Security & Fair-Play
  • RetroSki is all about skill and fun, using third-party software, cheats, or exploits is strictly prohibited.
  • If suspicious activity is detected, your rides may be invalidated, and repeated offenses could lead to permanent exclusion.
  • Respect your fellow riders, keep the competition friendly and the slopes fun for everyone.
Online events & server availability
  • Online events may be time-limited, make sure to check if an event is still open before jumping in.
  • Server availability may vary depending on demand, maintenance, or unexpected snowstorms.
  • If you experience connection issues, try again later or check our updates for server status.
