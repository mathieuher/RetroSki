Introduction
Retroski is a small game development project to peacefully ride the mountain and challenge your friends on randomly generated 2D alpine ski tracks.
Game modes
Ride local
Choose one of the existing track or generate a custom one and keep track of best rides on this device. This mode can also be played with your friends by sharing the device on each ride.
Ride online
Create or join a server with your friends to share tracks and challenge your best rides.
Controls
Retroski is pretty simple to play with very few controls but very difficult to master.
You can use different devices to ride the mountain.
Devices
Keyboard
Gamepad
Smartphone
Game setup
You can access to specific game setup by clicking on the top right corner.
  • Sounds
  • Ghosts (can affect performance)
  • Spectators (can affect performance)
You also have the possibility to restore the game to default setup and erase all data.

Quick tips
Stay on the track !
Don’t miss a gate or you’ll receive a 3sec. penalty.
Track styles
Retroski simulate the 4 alpine ski track styles with their specific tracks design and rider abilities.
Performances
Retroski should be playable on any modern device with an up to date browser. In case of performance trouble try to disabled ghosts and spectators from the setup menu.
